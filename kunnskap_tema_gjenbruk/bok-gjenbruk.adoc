= Reusability Factsheet Template (2015)

* Rapport: .link:https://joinup.ec.europa.eu/sites/default/files/document/2016-11/sc73_d02.03_reusability_factsheet_v4.00.pdf[Reusability Factsheet Template (2015)]
+
The purpose of this report is to propose a templatefor a factsheetthat wouldfacilitate the assessment of solutions reuseby providing usefuland detailed information that should be considered when evaluating reusing a solutionin a specific context.This factsheet should be consideredas complementary to the solution’s documentation available on Joinup’s catalogue of interoperability solutionsas well as on the EU Cartography. It is important to note that this factsheetfocuses on reusability of technical solutions both as a software component and as a service. Future work envisages the extension of the scope towards semantic, organisational and legal solutions.
